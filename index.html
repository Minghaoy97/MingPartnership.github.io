To Our Partners: Our Commitment to Value Investing

Ming Partnership Fund represents a pooled capital structure combining assets from our partners. As the General Partner, my role is to manage these assets according to Value Investment philosophy, driven by the mission of realizing significant, long-term capital appreciation for every partner.

Our investment strategy is solely focused on security investments and adheres to a zero-leverage policy. We prioritize long-term, sustainable value creation over rapid, short-term returns. This online platform plays a critical role in transparently recording our long-term performance and ensuring full disclosure to our partners.

Core Investment Tenets:

  • Business Focus
  • Long-term Oriented
  • Margin of Safety
