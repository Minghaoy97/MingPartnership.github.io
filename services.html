Seeking Wisdom: My Road to Investment

My lifelong role model, Mr. Charlie Munger, insightfully stated that "the art of stock picking is a subdivision of the art of worldly wisdom." This pursuit of worldly wisdom resonates deeply with the spirits of Chinese culture, which emphasizes the foundation of continuous self-cultivation. I am profoundly grateful for his enlightenment, which has guided my path.

I dedicate this page to recording my career in value investing—a path I view not merely as a financial endeavor, but as a lifelong journey in the seeking of wisdom.
